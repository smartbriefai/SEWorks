Who We Are

About SEW

Shanthi Engineering Works (SEW) is a Chennai-based engineering enterprise with over three decades of specialized experience in RADAR systems — from erection and assembly to full commissioning and long-term maintenance.

Founded by S. Rangasamy, SEW has built an unmatched reputation across India's most demanding sectors: national defense, space research, air traffic management, and meteorological surveillance. Our projects span remote mountain terrains, coastal installations, naval vessels, and urban high-rise environments.

As a trusted partner to institutions like ISRO, the Indian Navy, BEL, IMD, NAL, and the Indian Coast Guard, SEW brings government-grade rigor, field engineering excellence, and a commitment to mission-critical precision on every project.

We specialize not just in technical execution, but in the logistical complexity of deploying, relocating, and sustaining RADAR infrastructure across India's diverse and often challenging landscapes.

What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end RADAR engineering solutions — from steel erection to system handover — delivered across India's most demanding environments.

RADAR Erection & Assembly

Full structural erection of RADAR antenna towers, masts, and lattice steel frameworks. We handle foundation-to-apex assembly with precision alignment for optimal signal performance across all system types.

Radome Installation & Maintenance

Expert installation, alignment, and maintenance of protective radomes for all weather and defense RADAR systems. We ensure structural integrity and RF transparency throughout the radome lifecycle.

System Commissioning & Handover

Comprehensive end-to-end system commissioning including alignment verification, performance testing, calibration, and formal handover documentation. We ensure your RADAR system is operationally certified.

Dismantling & Relocation

Safe, systematic dismantling of RADAR installations for relocation or decommissioning. We preserve equipment integrity through structured disassembly, transport coordination, and reinstallation at new sites.

Air Traffic RADAR Systems

Installation and commissioning of Air Traffic Control RADAR systems at airports and defense airfields. We deliver precision-grade ASR and ARSR systems that meet the rigorous standards of DGCA and Indian Air Force.

Weather & Defence RADAR Systems

Specialized installation of Doppler weather RADAR (DWSR), C-band, S-band, and X-band systems for IMD and defense applications. We handle structural, RF, and systems integration for complete meteorological and surveillance capability.

RADAR antenna mounted on active lighthouse structure for Indian Coast Guard coastal monitoring. Saltwater environment hardening, structural compatibility analysis, and marine-grade commissioning.

Lattice steel tower erection on open terrain for long-range defense surveillance RADAR. Civil foundation, steel assembly, antenna mounting, and full systems integration with ground equipment shelter.

Large-aperture parabolic antenna installation for ISRO/IMD class applications. High-precision azimuth/elevation alignment, encoder calibration, and RF performance verification against design specifications.
